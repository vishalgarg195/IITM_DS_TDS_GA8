Financial Formula

Net Present Value (NPV):

\[ \mathrm{NPV} = \sum_{t=0}^{N} \frac{C_t}{(1+r)^t} \]

Weighted Average Cost of Capital (WACC):

\( \mathrm{WACC} = \frac{E}{V} r_E + \frac{D}{V} r_D (1 - T_c) \)
